Frequently Asked Questions

How are the funds managed?

Funds are held transparently in a Gnosis Safe multisig wallet managed by Giveth and the Lineum founder. All spending for the hackathon will be publicly documented and tracked on the blockchain for complete transparency.

What if you don't reach your goal?

We believe in the community! If we don't hit our initial goal, we will still host the hackathon with the amount raised and adjust prize tiers accordingly. Every dollar counts and goes directly toward empowering designers.

Who can participate in the hackathon?

Anyone - Developers, designers, and creators from all over the world. We encourage teams to form with both web3 devs and web2 designers to bridge the gap between traditional design and decentralized technology.

When will the hackathon take place?

The exact dates will be announced once we reach our funding milestone. We're planning on Nov/Dec 2025 to give participants ample time to prepare and build amazing decentralized design solutions.

How will I receive my donor rewards?

Rewards will be distributed automatically to the wallet address you use for donation. NFTs will be minted within 48 hours, Discord roles assigned immediately, and website recognition updated weekly.
